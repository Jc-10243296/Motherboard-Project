The CPU is also known as the Central Processing Unit. This provides the computer with processing power in order to do the tasks that it needs to do. It can be overclocked (or not) depending on what you need. If you want more speed, however, then overclocking the cpu can provide slightly better performance. Some fun facts is that present CPUS need about 582 million transistors in order to function
The capacitor is an electrical component that can store electrical charge even when there is nothing powering it such as a battery. It can only hold charge for a short amount of time, unlike a bettery.
RAM slots are used to put in your RAM. Ram stands for Random Access Memory, being the memory that you hold temporarily, whether it keep keeping the tabs you had open on your browser or for games that keep location. When you have one, it is best to put it on the slot closest to your cpu, and then if there are 2 then you have to add them to where they have the same color as they will be in the same channel
the CPU Fan Mount is used to hold your CPU Fan. This is used to cool down your cpu and prevent it from overheating
the ATX connectors are used as a power connector. This is your main source of power to the motherboard provided through the power supply.
the ROM BIOS is used to hold the standard settings of your device. This includes things such as the system clock and basic commands that are needed to start the computer. ROM stands for read only memory, which is your permanent memory
the CMOS battery is used as the battery for the ROM BIOS. When you leave your computer off for a long time, components will lose power. the CMOS is dedicated to the ROM BIOS so the ROM BIOS never runs out of power, at least for 2 years.
The CPU Power Connector is used to power the CPU. When CPUS become better, they may require more power, leading to them needing a dedicated power chord to power them. This is what this connector is for
the Fan Power Connectors are used to power the Fans. Depending on the amount of pins, they may be connected to different things. For example, the 3 pin one will likely power the case, while the 4 pin will power the CPU
Coils are used to maintain and regulate electrical energy inside of a motherboard. It can also be used to prevent power dips for your motherboard
the northBridge is used to aid the CPU in all of its functions. Particularly, it does the RAM and graphics, or PCIE slots.
The south bridge does everything that the north bridge doesn't. While the northbridge aids in ram and graphics, the southnridge focuses on things such as ports and the rest of the stuff that the northbridge doesn't that the cpu needs help with. Sometimes this chip can be seen wihtout a heatsink because it doesn't need it.
Input Ports are used to connect many other things to the motherboard. This can include things such as a personal keyboard and mouse to a HDMI cable that can display what the motherboard sends it to display.
the PCIE 16x is used to connect things such as dedicated graphic cards. It stands for Peripheral Component interconnect express. 16x demonstrates its size, as we'll see with the 1x one below
the PCI 16x is the slightly older version of PCIE. It is slightly slower but is still a PCI connector.
the PCI 1x is the smaller versions of both of the above PCI's. They provide the same function as the PCI 16x.
The buzzer is used when first starting up a computer to troubleshoot. It provides a number of buzzes which can be turned into error codes to find out what is wrong with your motherboard.
Sata slots are used to connect sata connectors, which are used to connect things such as hard drive, ssds, and other things into your motherboard to provide storage.
Jumper Pins are used to change the settings on a motherboard. when you connect pins through jumpers, you are changing settings on your motherboard.
A transistor is used to create logical decisions inside of a motherboard. The CPU hulds up to 582 million of them, but there are some that are used on the outside in order to perform tasks
Diode are used to send electricity in one direction. On motherboard, diodes are often covered up with a metal piece.
This display is a creative component, not used on motherboard. This display can be used to show certain errors (troubleshoot like the buzzer) or show an image, which ever you see fit
The status LED is a creative component. This is used similar to the display and the buzzer, but it can do the buzzers functions quicker. For example, if all lights blink, then you can add 3 instead of 1 because 3 leds blinked. (2 if 2 blinks, etc) you can also used binary if you had more of these LEDS.
